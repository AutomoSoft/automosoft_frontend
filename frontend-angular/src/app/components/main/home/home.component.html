Preethi Motors is one of the leading car repairing and maintenance garages in the Southern Province of Sri Lanka. It was founded by the Mr. Ranjan Preethi Palliyaguru, an automobile engineer on 26th of March 1982. At the inception of the business there were only 8 people to work and it was a small car repairing garage in a small plot of land. With the expertise of Mr. Preethi Palliyaguru and his strong public relations, he was able to expand the garage into a large scale car repairing center with a 3 storied building and a larger space in the mid of the Matara town area in 1998. Moreover, he expanded his business through opening up a spare part trading shop and also by entering into public transportation sector. Preethi Motors is regarded as an “A grade” garage and it also operates in issuing valuation reports and fitness reports. Preethi Motors comprises a labour force of 40 people.
